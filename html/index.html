Best practices and resources for embedding accessibility into your workflow.

Nau mai, haere mai

Accessibility for MSD builds upon the Accessibility Charter and the New Zealand Government Web Standards(this link opens an external website). Everyone has a role to play to make our information and services more accessible.

Building in accessibility from the start is key to ensuring that your programs, service, tools and applications can be used by everyone.

Accessible content presents information through multi-sensory channels. Allowing extra means of navigation and interactivity beyond the typical point-and-click interface.
